Parma
Main LZ for Skyport and EJ launches

Altitude: 751ft
Rating: P2/H2

If you have not landed Parma yet, contact local pilots on our Telegram chat to arrange for a site introduction. 

There are two main sections for landing: 
	-Lower Parma can easily be spotted from Skyport because of its recognizable triangular shape. It is the main landing area for hang gliders before East Beach. You will land on a slope. Be ready to use your side landing skills.
	-Upper Parma is great for landing in South to West winds but has more landscaping features like rocks and sprinklers.

The usual meeting point for driving up to mountain launches is at the main  Parma Park Entrance on Stanwood.
