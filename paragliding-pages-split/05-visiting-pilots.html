Visiting Pilots


Welcome Visiting Pilots!


Flying Santa Barbara is a unique experience. Best learning conditions at Elings Flight Park, great coastal soaring sites and a south facing mountain range conducive to XC flying allowing for transitions to other ranges.


There is a $30 fee to become a member of SBSA. It will give you access to our Telegram group chat and will be used to contribute to site maintenance, site preservation, our website, community building events, trips to other flying sites and our rainy days fund.


Pay SBSA Membership

  • Join our Telegram Group Chat and ask for local advice
  • Check out our site guidelines and NoLZ 
  • Fill out the waiver form
  • If you're going XC, you should install our DNL file. It is sensitive and only shared with visitors upon request - hop on Telegram, introduce yourself and someone will gladly share it with you.
  • Have fun!
