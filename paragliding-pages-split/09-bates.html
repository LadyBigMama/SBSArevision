Bates


Coastal Ridge Soaring



Rating: P2/H2

Wind direction: S/SW

Radio Frequency: 144.310



Wonderful Ridge soaring site in Carpinteria for all levels. Large dirt Launch. Landing on the beach or top landing


Pay attention to white caps over the ocean indicating strong wind coming onshore. Too much wind could push you over the freeway or over the water depending on the direction.



FYI Viola Park located West of Bates is a great practice field for kiting
